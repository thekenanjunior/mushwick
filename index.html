About Me

Hello, I'm MushWick!

I, (MushWick) was born on 26 March, 1987, in Baku, Azerbaijan. I started to work in media as a video editor at PicMus Production in 2013. Later, I studied directing at My Film School. After that, in 2016, I recorded my first filmmaker job. In same year, I served as director assistant of a TV Series at Xezer TV. From 2017, I started to work as filmmaker and videographer and recorded lots of videos, clips, and advertisements. Since the beginning of 2018, my team and I have been working under a company I founded called Apeiron Group. I also work as a freelance videographer and photographer.
